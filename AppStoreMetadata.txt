📖 DESCRIPTION (PLAIN TEXT ONLY, NO EMOJIS, NO BULLET POINTS):
Baby Journey is the only app you need from the moment you see two lines on the test until your toddler takes their first steps. The app seamlessly covers both pregnancy tracking and baby milestone recording in one beautiful, intuitive experience.

During pregnancy, Baby Journey guides you week by week with delightful fruit size comparisons so you always know how big your little one is growing. Each of the 40 weeks includes detailed development information about what is happening inside, along with practical tips tailored to that specific stage of pregnancy. You will never miss an important medical appointment thanks to the built-in checkup tracker that covers every key test and scan from the first visit through delivery day.

The weight tracker lets you monitor your pregnancy weight gain with a clean line chart that shows your trend over time. The kick counter provides a simple tap-to-count interface with a built-in timer, helping you follow the recommended daily kick counting practice. When it is time to pack for the hospital, the categorized hospital bag checklist ensures you have everything organized from documents to baby essentials.

After your baby arrives, Baby Journey transitions into a comprehensive milestone tracker covering motor skills, speech development, social interactions, and cognitive growth from birth through 24 months. Each milestone shows the typical age range so you know what to expect and when. The growth charts track weight, length, and head circumference with visual line graphs that make it easy to see your baby's progress at a glance.

All data is stored securely on your device with no accounts or internet connection required. You can export your complete journey as a JSON file to keep a backup or share with your partner. Baby Journey is designed with a warm, calming dark interface that is gentle on tired eyes during those late-night feedings.
